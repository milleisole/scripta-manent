## English

### Part 1: Connect Your Google Drive

Before using Scripta Manent, you need to allow the app to access your Google Drive. This is a one-time setup.

**Step 1: Open the app**

Open Scripta Manent in your browser. You'll see a "Connect to Google Drive" button.

**Step 2: Sign in with Google**

Click the button. A Google sign-in window will appear. Choose the Google account where you want to store your files.

**Step 3: Grant permission**

Google will ask if you want to allow Scripta Manent to access your Drive. Click "Allow".

The app can only see the files it creates — it cannot access your other documents, photos, or anything else in your Drive.

**Step 4: You're done!**

The app will create a folder called `scripta-manent` in your Drive. All your notes and files will be stored there.

---

### Part 2: Using the App

**Creating a note**

1. Tap the "+" button
2. Choose "New Note"
3. Write your note — you can use plain text or Markdown
4. The note saves automatically

**Uploading a file**

1. Tap the "+" button
2. Choose "Upload File"
3. Select the file from your device
4. Done — the file is now in your Drive

**Organizing with folders**

1. Go to the folder view
2. Tap "New Folder"
3. Name your folder
4. Drag files into it, or move them using the menu

**Searching**

Use the search bar at the top. It searches through:
- Note titles
- Note content
- File names
- Tags

**Encrypting sensitive content**

1. Open a note or file
2. Tap the lock icon
3. If this is your first encrypted item, you'll set a master password
4. The content is now encrypted — only you can read it

**Sharing**

1. Open the file you want to share
2. Tap the share icon
3. Choose:
   - **QR Code**: Show a code others can scan to download
   - **Share**: Opens your phone's share menu (email, WhatsApp, etc.)

---

### Part 3: Install as App (PWA)

You can install Scripta Manent on your phone or computer for quick access.

**On iPhone/iPad (Safari):**
1. Open the app in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the app in Chrome
2. Tap the three dots menu (⋮)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4. Tap "Install"

**On Computer (Chrome/Edge):**
1. Open the app in your browser
2. Look for the install icon in the address bar (⊕)
3. Click "Install"

Now you can open Scripta Manent like any other app!
